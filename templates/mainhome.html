OUR MISSION

Our mission is to deliver high quality, affordable healthcare services to the broader population in India. Our core values are represented by the acronym "iCare", which encompasses innovation and efficiency, Compassionate care, Accountability, Respect for all, and Excellence as a culture. At the same time, we seek to generate a strong financial performance and deliver long-term value to our shareholders through the execution of our business strategy



ABOUT US

Page Healthcare, Inc. is a private not-for-profit corporation that is proud to offer quality health care to the residents of Lee County, Florida. This organization oversees the operations of All About Home Care, Inc., a home health agency. The organization also oversees the operations of Page Healthcare Foundation, which accepts donations to directly benefit the patients served by the agency. The Board of Directors and management team of Page Healthcare, Inc. are dedicated to maintaining a high level of customer service through a continuum of care that includes nursing care as well as in-home services. The Board is made up of volunteer members of the community that have experience in various backgrounds including healthcare, legal, engineering, financial, education, and political areas. Their experience along with the extensive health care and financial experience provided by the management team has allowed the organization to continue to grow and provide quality, progressive health care.
